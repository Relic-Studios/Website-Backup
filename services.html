What We Do

We create immersive visual experiences for live events, festivals, and brands. From concept to execution, on-site or remote.

01

Live VJ Performance

Real-time visual performances synchronized with music. We bring the visuals, the hardware knowledge, and the instinct to read a crowd.

  • Real-time visuals via TouchDesigner / Resolume
  • Audio-reactive content synced to DJ sets
  • On-site VJ operation for your event
  • Multi-screen and LED wall support
02

Pre-Rendered Content

Custom motion graphics packages designed for your event's visual identity. Delivered in any resolution, any aspect ratio, ready to play.

  • Custom loops and transitions
  • Branded visual packages
  • 4K / 8K LED wall content
  • Cinema 4D, After Effects, Notch
03

Interactive Installations

Sensor-driven, crowd-reactive visual systems that respond to movement, sound, and environment in real time.

  • Motion-tracked reactive visuals
  • Projection mapping
  • Custom TouchDesigner systems
  • Immersive spatial experiences
04

AI-Driven Visuals

Cutting-edge generative art and AI-assisted visual workflows. We build custom pipelines that produce visuals no one else can.

  • Custom Stable Diffusion pipelines
  • Real-time AI visual generation
  • ComfyUI workflow development
  • Generative art for events and brands
05

Brand & Commercial

Motion graphics for brands, product launches, and commercial campaigns. Visuals that move with purpose and tell your story.

  • Brand identity motion packages
  • Product launch visuals
  • Social media content
  • Commercial animation
06

Technical Consulting

Already have a vision but need the technical expertise? We consult on LED systems, visual workflows, and real-time graphics pipelines.

  • LED wall spec and setup guidance
  • Visual system architecture
  • Workflow optimization
  • Team training and mentorship
